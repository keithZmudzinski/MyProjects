About
Quin69 is a professional Diablo 3 streamer, who has consistently competed for and achieved the title of #1 monk in the world. Quin has been an active member of the Diablo community for several years, and has amassed a large amount of information about Diablo through his plethora of painstakingly thorough guides and videos. In addition, he streams regularly as a way to connect to his audience as well as act like an ass in front of thousands of people.This site is dedicated to providing a complete list of all his current and past builds, as a resource for the community to use.
